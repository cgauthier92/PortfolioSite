I'm Chad Gauthier a 24 year old Web Developer currently residing in Toronto. I'm bilingual, have a background in art and a passion for merging beautiful design with responsive code. I graduated from Ocad U and BrainStation and am currently working towards a career as a front-end developer.

I'm mainly involved in writing web applications in JavaScript. I have experience with CSS (SCSS), Jquery and Angular.js. Using these frameworks and Libraries I build fast, interactive client-side websites. Once the proper information architecture is in place, I design the visual layout to create a beautiful user experience. I enjoy the planning and iterating of a site's structural layout just as much as I love the coding behind it.
I'm a graduate from the Web Development Immersive bootcamp at BrainStation, as well as have a BFA in Drawing and Painting from Ocad U. I believe that attention to detail, pragmatism, and correctness of style (both in coding and in markup) are equally important in any project. In web development in particular, usability guidelines and best practices exist for a good reason. Currently working on a site for Culture Kids Canada using Angular.js to help promote their publication about Art and cultural heritage within Canada.
